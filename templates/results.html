A montage in EEG recording refers to the specific arrangement of electrodes on the scalp, and the way the electrical signals are referenced and displayed. Different montages can highlight different aspects of brain activity, making it easier to identify specific patterns or anomalies.

Our platform provides a selection of montages derived from the Temple University Hospital (TUH) EEG Corpus, one of the largest and most comprehensive datasets for EEG research. By choosing an appropriate montage, you can tailor the predictions and visualization to better suit your analysis needs. Once you have selected a montage, click the "Predict for Seizure" button to proceed with the analysis based on your chosen configuration.
